What makes this a “microflow”

  • Combines steps: Service + Location (was 2 screens) → 1 screen. Date + Time picker with smart defaults → 1 screen.
  • Smart defaults: Prefills the earliest available date and preselects the first open slot after user picks a date.
  • Inline review: Shows a review card before confirmation to avoid a separate “review” page.
  • Keyboard & screen‑reader friendly: Focus styles, ARIA labels, and clear headings.
